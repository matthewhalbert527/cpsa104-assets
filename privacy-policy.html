Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

CPSA Detroit District Chapter 104 uses this website to share chapter information, member artwork, membership details, and contact options. This policy explains what information may be collected and how it may be used.

Information you choose to send

If you email the chapter or use the contact form, your name, email address, topic, and message are sent through your email client to the chapter contact. We use that information to respond to your inquiry, answer membership questions, and share relevant chapter information.

Advertising and cookies

This site may use Google AdSense to show advertising. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a visitor's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also learn about choices for some third-party advertising cookies at aboutads.info.

Website files and logs

The website host may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, requested pages, referring pages, and timestamps to deliver the site, protect the service, and understand aggregate usage.

External links

This site links to external services, including CPSA national membership resources and membership form hosting. Those sites have their own privacy practices and are not controlled by CPSA Detroit #104.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@cpsa104.org.
